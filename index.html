Data-driven Astronomy Expedition

Launching Your Celestial Journey

Workshop, Indian Institute Of Technology - Hyderabad, 2023


Organized by IUCAA and IIT Hyderabad

Workshop Poster

About the Wokshop: Modern day telescopes produce petabytes of data and this presents students with the opportunity to analyze such publicly available astronomical data sets. This workshop is aimed at providing early bachelors/late masters students having basic knowledge of physics, mathematics and computation with a hands-on introduction to astronomy using such datasets. This 3 day workshop will cover some of the basic concepts of stellar, galactic and extra-galactic physics through short lectures and hands-on computational sessions with some of the publicly available astronomical datasets. Students will be provided with food and accommodation for their interval of stay.
